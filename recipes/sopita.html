Sopita

Decription

This hispanic homey dish is a comforting warm meal perfect for eating during any season with anyone, and it only takes about 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Shell, Fideo, or Pasta of Choice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tomatoes
  • White onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Water

Steps

  1. In a blender add 1 1/2 tomatoes, a clove of garlic, a small piece of white onion, and chicken bouillion to taste.
  2. Blend with a cup of water until smooth and set aside for later use.
  3. Heat up your pot over medium heat and pour enough vegetable oil to cover the bottom of the pot with a think layer of oil.
  4. Once hot, add in your pasta of choice and stir until they're a nice golden brown. Make sure to frequently stir otherwise the pasta will burn.
  5. Once golden brown, add in your sauce. Stir and let cook for about a minute.
  6. Add the rest of the water into the pot and taste for chicken bouillion.
  7. Bring to a boil then reduce the heat to medium low and cover.
  8. Let it cook for about 10 minutes or until the pasta is nice and soft
  9. Remove from the heat and Enjoy!
